A general purpose PC fan controller. 6 fan ports, 2 I^2C temperature sensors, controlled via USB. Firmware written with avr-libc and LUFA

My custom controlling software is certified to be 95% less likely to stop working spontaneously or sporadically crash your computer than Corsair's iCUE software.
